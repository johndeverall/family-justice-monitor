Urgent Support

If you are currently experiencing a family justice matter and need immediate support, please contact:

  • Citizens Advice Bureau: 0800 367 222
  • Women's Refuge: 0800 733 843
  • KidzNeedDadz: 0800 543 996
  • Shine (family violence support): 0508 744 633
  • Community Law Centres: communitylaw.org.nz
  • Your local legal aid provider

Family Violence Programme Providers

For a comprehensive list of government-funded family violence safety and non-violence programme providers in your area, visit the Ministry of Justice Family Violence Service Providers directory.

We are a research and advocacy organisation and cannot provide legal advice or direct case support.

Office Location
